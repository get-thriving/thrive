# Vision

A _Vision_ is a free-form document that captures who you want to be and what
you want your life to look like. It is the highest-level, most personal part
of the [Life Plan](overview.md).

## Purpose

The Vision is not a plan or a to-do list. It is a narrative or a set of
reflections — a description of the kind of life you are aiming to build.
Think of it as the answer to: _"If everything went well, what would my life
look and feel like?"_

Having a written Vision gives your goals, chapters, and daily work a sense of
direction and meaning. The periodic [evaluation](overview.md#periodic-evaluation)
workflow is designed to keep the Vision alive and up to date.

## Status

A Vision moves through three statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
| ------ | ------- |
| **Draft** | Being written, not yet the guiding vision. |
| **Active** | The current, guiding vision for your life. |
| **Old** | A past vision, kept for reference. |

There is at most one _Active_ vision at any time. When you mark a draft as
active, any previously active vision is automatically moved to _Old_.

## Content

A Vision has a single **note** attached to it, which is where you write its
actual content. Thrive does not impose any structure on this note — use
whatever format works best for you (prose, bullet points, sections for
different life domains, etc.).

## Workflow

1. Create a new Vision — it starts in **Draft** status.
2. Write your vision in the attached note.
3. When ready, mark it as **Active**. This becomes your current guiding
   vision.
4. Over time, create new drafts to reflect how your thinking evolves. When a
   new draft is ready, promote it to active and the old one is archived.

## Accessing Visions

Open the **Life Plan** section from the sidebar, then choose **Vision** from
the menu. You can view your current active vision, browse past visions, and
create new drafts.
