# Life Weeks

The _Life Weeks_ view visualises your entire life as a grid of small cells —
one cell per week. The grid has **52 columns** (one per week of the year) and
**100 rows** (one per year of life), giving 5 200 cells in total.

Each cell is coloured to show where that week sits relative to today:

| Colour      | Meaning             |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Golden      | The current week    |
| Gray        | A week in the past  |
| Light gray  | A week in the future|

The calculation is based on the birthday stored in your
[Life Plan](../overview.md) settings (`birthday` and `birth_year` fields).
Row 0 starts at your date of birth; row 99 ends 100 years after it.

## Accessing the View

Open the **Life Plan** section from the sidebar, then choose **Life Weeks**
from the compact action menu in the top-right corner of the page. The view
opens as a side panel that can be expanded to different widths.

## Home Page Widget

A compact version of the Life Weeks grid is available as a home page widget
called **Life Weeks**. It uses 2 × 2 px cells so the full 100 × 52 grid fits
in a 1 × 1 widget slot. Add it from the home page tab editor.

The widget requires the _Life Plan_ workspace feature to be enabled.
